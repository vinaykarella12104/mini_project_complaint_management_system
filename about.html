Online Complaint Management System

About

The Online Complaint Management System is designed to streamline the process of handling complaints efficiently and effectively. It allows users to submit complaints online, track their status, and provides administrators with tools to manage and resolve complaints.

Features

  • Easy online submission of complaints
  • Real-time tracking of complaint status
  • Notification system for updates
  • Admin dashboard for managing complaints
  • Secure and confidential handling of complaints

How It Works

  1. User submits a complaint via the online form.
  2. Complaint is assigned a unique tracking number.
  3. Admin reviews and assigns the complaint to relevant personnel.
  4. Personnel investigate and resolve the complaint.
  5. User receives updates on the complaint status.
  6. Complaint is marked resolved upon successful resolution.
